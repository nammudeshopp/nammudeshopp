Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Thank you for shopping with NammudeShopp. This policy outlines the terms for cancellations and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order.
  • Once the order has been processed or shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

2. No Return Policy

  • We do not accept returns for any products once they are delivered.
  • All sales are considered final.

3. Refunds

Refunds will only be provided in the following cases:

  • If the product is damaged during transit
  • If you receive a wrong item

To request a refund, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery with proof (photos/videos).

If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days
  • The amount will be credited to the original payment method

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Incorrect address provided by the customer
  • Failure to accept delivery

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about cancellations or refunds, please contact us:

  • Email: nammudeshopp@gmail.com

By placing an order on our website, you agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.
