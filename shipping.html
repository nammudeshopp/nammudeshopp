Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Thank you for shopping with NammudeShopp. This Shipping & Delivery Policy explains how we handle order processing, shipping, and delivery.

1. Order Processing

  • All orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Orders are not processed or shipped on Sundays or public holidays.
  • In case of delays due to high order volume, customers will be informed via email.

2. Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your delivery location.
  • We may offer free shipping on selected products or promotional offers.

3. Delivery Time

  • Estimated delivery time is 3–7 business days across India.
  • Delivery times may vary depending on your location and courier service.
  • Unexpected delays may occur due to weather, logistics issues, or other external factors.

4. Shipping Partners

  • We use trusted courier partners to deliver your orders safely.
  • Once your order is shipped, a tracking ID will be shared via email.

5. Order Tracking

You can track your order using the tracking details provided after dispatch.

6. Delivery Issues

  • If your order is delayed, damaged, or lost, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • We will assist you in resolving the issue as quickly as possible.

7. Incorrect Address

  • Please ensure that the shipping address is accurate.
  • We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping and delivery, please contact us:

  • Email: nammudeshopp@gmail.com

By placing an order on our website, you agree to this Shipping & Delivery Policy.
